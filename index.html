Shradha Sharma

Founder & CEO, YourStory

About

Shradha founded YourStory in 2008 with one goal—tell stories of India's new-age entrepreneurs. Over the last 16 years, YourStory has been the champion, the chronicler, and the catalyst for each changemaker in the country dreaming and daring to tread the road to entrepreneurship.

YourStory's Impact

  • Touched over 10 million lives across 20+ digital assets such as TechSparks and SheSparks
  • Editorial coverage in 11+ languages and across sectors
  • Launched subscription-based premium platform The CapTable

Vision

Shradha has committed to the bold vision of creating 5 million job-creators by 2028, including more women job creators through practical, outcome-based courses, for entrepreneurship success and growth.
